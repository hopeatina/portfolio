# Receipt inbox

Drop thoughts here anytime — one bullet per thought. The daily receipt routine reads
this file, weaves your thinking into the draft, then moves what it used into
`receipts/archive/YYYY-MM-DD.md` inside the same PR. Anything left here carries over.

Good inbox notes: a decision you made and why, a number you observed, something that
broke, a suspicion you want tested, an angle for the story. Fragments are fine —
the routine's job is the weaving, yours is the judgment.
