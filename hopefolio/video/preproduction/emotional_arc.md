# Emotional arc — Continuity Signal

## Intent

Make Hope feel like the rare engineer who can hold both system complexity and human judgment. The film should open with a productive question, accelerate through visible proof, then resolve on Hope's face and a memorable point of view.

## Non-negotiables

- Hope's face appears in the opening and the final resolution.
- Portfolio UI remains legible; no generic device mockup wallpaper.
- Every numeric claim is already present in the portfolio source.
- No voiceover is required: the edit must work muted with burned-in type and captions.
- Motion stays physical and editorial: crops, planes, reveals, and scroll translation—not decorative particle effects.
